What are the 5 R's?

The 5 R's represent a hierarchy of actions to reduce waste and manage resources more sustainably. Each R prioritizes different actions:

  • Refuse: Say no to unnecessary items and packaging.
  • Reduce: Minimize consumption and waste generation.
  • Reuse: Use items multiple times or repurpose them.
  • Recycle: Collect materials to be used as raw materials for new products.
  • Rot: Compost organic waste to return nutrients to the soil.

Practical Tips for Each R

Refuse

Say no to single-use plastics and unnecessary packaging. Bring your own reusable bags and containers.

Reduce

Buy only what you need, and choose products with minimal packaging. Opt for digital copies whenever possible.

Reuse

Reuse containers, bottles, and jars for storage. Donate or repurpose items you no longer need.

Recycle

Sort and recycle materials like paper, plastics, glass, and metals according to local recycling guidelines.

Rot

Compost fruit and vegetable scraps to create nutrient-rich soil for gardening.
